Getting here

Sitnl Beginn: 05.06.2024 8:00AM Ende: 05.06.2024 6:00PM Location: SAP Nederland, Amerikastraat 10, 5232BE 's Hertogenbosch', The Netherlands

By Car

Coming by A2 from the North

  • Take exit 20-Rosmalen from the A2.
  • At the roundabout take the 3rd exit to the Reitscheweg
  • Turn left onto Amerikastraat
  • The SAP office will be on your right

Coming by A2 from the South

  • Take exit 20-Rosmalen from the A2.
  • Turn left at the traffic lights
  • At the next traffic lights, turn left again
  • Drive over the A2 and turn left again
  • At the roundabout take the 3rd exit to the Reitscheweg
  • Turn left onto Amerikastraat
  • The SAP office will be on your right

Parking

The parking deck of the SAP office has ample parking space available. Parking is free.

By Public Transport

From 's Hertogenbosch train station:

  • Take bus 6 in the direction of Empel
  • Exit at Europalaan, the 9th stop
  • Walk to the SAP office (2 min, 160m)

or

  • Take bus 4 in the direction of Rosmalen
  • Exit at De Reit, the 9th stop
  • Walk to the SAP office (9 min, 700m)
